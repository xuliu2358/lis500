Welcome to our project website!

Welcome to our project website! Here we explore the connections between technology and social issues, particularly regarding inequality and prejudice. Through our research, resources, and personal insights, you'll learn how technology affects society, and specifically its impact on these complex social issues.

We also explore important concepts such as implicit bias and intersectionality in hopes of inspiring and helping students who are approaching these topics for the first time. Embark on this learning journey together and discover the heroes and heroines who are defying the norm in the field of technology!
